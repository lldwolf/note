第N章 WPF Ribbon 组件

以Excel为例，Ribbon组件包括以下内容：

  • Page: 包括上例中的Home, Insert, ...，Page 包括标题和脚注
  • Button: Page 中的每个元素
  • 工具栏
  • Application Menu，在上例为 File 页
  • 与之配套的底端状态栏

创建 Ribbon 组件

首先创建一个不带任何内容的 Ribbon 示例

XAML

<Window x:Class="XpfDemo.RibbonWindow"
		xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml/presentation"
		xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml"
		xmlns:dxb="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/bars"
		xmlns:dxr="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/ribbon"
		Title="RibbonWindow"
		Height="488"
		Width="914">
	<dxb:BarManager>
		<Grid>
			<Grid.RowDefinitions>
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
				<RowDefinition Height="*" />
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
			</Grid.RowDefinitions>
			<dxr:RibbonControl>

			</dxr:RibbonControl>
			<Image Grid.Row="1"></Image>
			<dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl Grid.Row="2">

			</dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl>
		</Grid>
	</dxb:BarManager>
</Window>

可以为 RibbonControl 设置如下属性

属性 说明 默认值
RibbonStyle 样式，可设为Office2007 或 Office2010 Office2007
ToolbarShowMode 工具栏的显示位置 Default，显示在顶部
PageCategoryAlignment Page 内容的对齐方式 Default

创建工具栏

XAML

<Window x:Class="XpfDemo.RibbonWindow"
		xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml/presentation"
		xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml"
		xmlns:dxb="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/bars"
		xmlns:dxr="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/ribbon"
		Title="RibbonWindow"
		Height="488"
		Width="914">
	<dxb:BarManager>
	
		<dxb:BarManager.Items>
			<dxb:BarButtonItem x:Name="toolbtn1"
							   Glyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img1.png"
							   Content="Button1" />
		</dxb:BarManager.Items>
		
		<Grid>
			<Grid.RowDefinitions>
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
				<RowDefinition Height="*" />
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
			</Grid.RowDefinitions>
			<dxr:RibbonControl RibbonStyle="Office2010"
							   ToolbarShowMode="Default"
							   PageCategoryAlignment="Default">
							   
				<dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
					<dxb:BarButtonItemLink BarItemName="toolbtn1" />
				</dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
				
			</dxr:RibbonControl>
			<Image Grid.Row="1"></Image>
			<dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl Grid.Row="2">
			</dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl>
		</Grid>
	</dxb:BarManager>
</Window>

创建 Application Menu

XAML

<Window x:Class="XpfDemo.RibbonWindow"
		xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml/presentation"
		xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml"
		xmlns:dxb="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/bars"
		xmlns:dxr="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/ribbon"
		Title="RibbonWindow"
		Height="488"
		Width="914">
	<dxb:BarManager>
		<dxb:BarManager.Items>
			<dxb:BarButtonItem x:Name="toolbtn1"
							   Glyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img1.png"
							   Content="Button1" />
							   
			<dxb:BarButtonItem x:Name="appMenuItem1"
							   Content="App Menu Item1"
							   LargeGlyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img2.png" />
							   
		</dxb:BarManager.Items>
		<Grid>
			<Grid.RowDefinitions>
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
				<RowDefinition Height="*" />
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
			</Grid.RowDefinitions>
			<dxr:RibbonControl RibbonStyle="Office2010"
							   ToolbarShowMode="Default"
							   PageCategoryAlignment="Default">
				<dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
					<dxb:BarButtonItemLink BarItemName="toolbtn1" />
				</dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
				
				<dxr:RibbonControl.ApplicationMenu>
					<dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo>
						<dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo.ItemLinks>
							<dxb:BarButtonItemLink BarItemName="appMenuItem1" />
						</dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo.ItemLinks>
					</dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo>
				</dxr:RibbonControl.ApplicationMenu>
				
			</dxr:RibbonControl>
			<Image Grid.Row="1"></Image>
			<dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl Grid.Row="2">
			</dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl>
		</Grid>
	</dxb:BarManager>
</Window>

创建 Page

XAML

<Window x:Class="XpfDemo.RibbonWindow"
		xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml/presentation"
		xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml"
		xmlns:dxb="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/bars"
		xmlns:dxr="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/ribbon"
		xmlns:dxe="http://schemas.devexpress.com/winfx/2008/xaml/editors"
		Title="RibbonWindow"
		Height="488"
		Width="914">
	<dxb:BarManager>
		<dxb:BarManager.Items>
			<dxb:BarButtonItem x:Name="toolbtn1"
							   Glyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img1.png"
							   Content="Button1" />
			<dxb:BarButtonItem x:Name="appMenuItem1"
							   Content="App Menu Item1"
							   LargeGlyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img2.png" />

			<dxb:BarEditItem x:Name="colorEdit"
							 Content="Tool Color"
							 EditWidth="100">
				<dxb:BarEditItem.EditSettings>
					<dxe:PopupColorEditSettings />
				</dxb:BarEditItem.EditSettings>
			</dxb:BarEditItem>
			<dxb:BarCheckItem x:Name="checkItem"
							  Content="Brush Tool"
							  Glyph="/XpfDemo;component/Images/img1.png" />

		</dxb:BarManager.Items>
		<Grid>
			<Grid.RowDefinitions>
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
				<RowDefinition Height="*" />
				<RowDefinition Height="Auto" />
			</Grid.RowDefinitions>
			<dxr:RibbonControl RibbonStyle="Office2010"
							   ToolbarShowMode="Default"
							   PageCategoryAlignment="Default">
				<dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
					<dxb:BarButtonItemLink BarItemName="toolbtn1" />
				</dxr:RibbonControl.ToolbarItemLinks>
				<dxr:RibbonControl.ApplicationMenu>
					<dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo>
						<dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo.ItemLinks>
							<dxb:BarButtonItemLink BarItemName="appMenuItem1" />
						</dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo.ItemLinks>
					</dxr:ApplicationMenuInfo>
				</dxr:RibbonControl.ApplicationMenu>

				<dxr:RibbonDefaultPageCategory>
					<dxr:RibbonPage Caption="Home">
						<dxr:RibbonPageGroup Caption="Tools">
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarItemLinkSeparator />
							<dxb:BarCheckItemLink BarItemName="checkItem" />
							<dxb:BarCheckItemLink BarItemName="checkItem" />
						</dxr:RibbonPageGroup>
					</dxr:RibbonPage>
				</dxr:RibbonDefaultPageCategory>

			</dxr:RibbonControl>
			<Image Grid.Row="1"></Image>
			<dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl Grid.Row="2">
			</dxr:RibbonStatusBarControl>
		</Grid>
	</dxb:BarManager>
</Window>

默认情况下，Ribbon Page 纵向可容纳3行控件，若填满则自动生成到下一列。

RibbonDefaultPageCategory 为默认的 Page 容器，Page 则为 容器。也可以创建其它的 RibbonPageCategory，如下所示：

XAML

				<dxr:RibbonPageCategory Caption="Appearance">
					<dxr:RibbonPage Caption="Page1">
						<dxr:RibbonPageGroup Caption="Group1">
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarEditItemLink BarItemName="colorEdit" />
							<dxb:BarItemLinkSeparator />
							<dxb:BarCheckItemLink BarItemName="checkItem" />
							<dxb:BarCheckItemLink BarItemName="checkItem" />
						</dxr:RibbonPageGroup>
					</dxr:RibbonPage>
				</dxr:RibbonPageCategory>
