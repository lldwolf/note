布局时常用效果

Splitter

类似于 WinForm 中的Splitter控件，可实现通过拖拽实现尺寸调整。示例代码如下

<Grid>
    <Grid.ColumnDefinitions>
        <ColumnDefinition Width="150"></ColumnDefinition>
        <ColumnDefinition Width="Auto"></ColumnDefinition>
        <ColumnDefinition></ColumnDefinition>
    </Grid.ColumnDefinitions>
    <Grid.RowDefinitions>
        <RowDefinition Height="25"></RowDefinition>
        <RowDefinition></RowDefinition>
    </Grid.RowDefinitions>
    <TextBox Grid.ColumnSpan="3"></TextBox>
    <TextBox Grid.Row="1"></TextBox>
    <GridSplitter Grid.Row="1" Grid.Column="1" HorizontalAlignment="Center" Width="5" ShowsPreview="True" VerticalAlignment="Stretch"></GridSplitter>
    <TextBox Grid.Row="1" Grid.Column="2"></TextBox>
</Grid>
