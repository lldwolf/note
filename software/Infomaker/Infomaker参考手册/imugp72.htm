Chapter 8 Highlighting Information in Reports and Forms

About this chapter

This chapter describes how you modify the way information displays in your reports and forms based on the conditions you specify. The conditions are usually related to data values, which are not available until execution time.

Most of the discussions in this chapter focus on reports. But you can also use the techniques described in this chapter in the Form painter to highlight information in forms.
