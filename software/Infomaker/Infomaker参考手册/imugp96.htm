Previewing crosstabs

Once you have defined the crosstab, you can see it with data in the Preview view.

To preview the crosstab:

  1. Select View>Preview from the menu bar to display the Preview view.

  2. Click on the Preview view to be sure it's current.

  3. Select Rows>Retrieve from the menu bar.

    InfoMaker retrieves the rows and performs the cross tabulation on the data.

    The Retrieve on Preview option makes retrieval happen automatically If the crosstab definition specifies Retrieve on Preview, retrieval happens automatically when the Preview view first displays.

  4. Continue enhancing your report and retrieve again when necessary to see the results of your enhancements.
